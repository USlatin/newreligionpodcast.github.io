INTRO

Welcome to New Religion! Join us as we explore the seismic implications of what happens when our reality is forced to expand.

Help us navigate the profound shifts in belief and meaning that lie ahead for humanity. If disclosure is going to happen, it is likely a two-way street, requiring the release of withheld information preceded by a readiness of society to accept it.

CONTENT

New Religion is a place for experts—theologians, researchers, and thinkers—to share their deepest insights on the impact disclosure would have on religion and society. We’ll share the full, unedited long-form podcasts and compile clips by subject from different episodes into deep-dive video essays.

We believe our higher power has a sense of humor, and we intend to remain playfully curious—balancing respect for the subject with enjoyment of this consequential, in-depth dialogue on the future of human reality.

ABOUT

Hosted by Rob Lequeux, a Los Angeles-based content creator and producer, New Religion is a space for objective and humble curiosity with a desire for personal spiritual growth. Our goal is to be the "last mile" in the dissemination of deep thoughts regarding the implications that disclosure would have on our foundational beliefs.

If you are interested in the deeper questions of who we are and what is unfolding in our world, please subscribe and join the conversation. We are just getting started.
